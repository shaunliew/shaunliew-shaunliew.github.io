The competitions that I joined so far

Hilti IT Competition 2021- Top 6 finalists

This is my first ever IT Competition in my lifetime. I wouldn't make this far without my teammates(Jackson, Iris, and Zhen Jun), our mentors, and everyone who helped us. This competition has definitely improved my skills in the CS field.

Our project, ProTime, is a system that uses artificial intelligence to predict an accurate timeline for construction projects to mitigate the problems of delay and communication. The system does this by utilising information about the construction project (type, manpower level, supply chain etc) as well as predicts the number of COVID-19 cases to curate timelines for each individual project.
