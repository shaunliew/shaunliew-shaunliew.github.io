About Me

Hello World. I'm Shaun Liew Xin Hong from Universiti Sains Malaysia, Penang.

I'm a Computer Science student minoring in Mathematics and I'm planning to take an AI major in Year 2. I love machine learning and AI. My passion drives me to learn more about the AI field by self-learning. To date, I have learned quite a number things about Machine Learning algorithms such as Linear Regression, K Nearest Neighbours, and Logistic Regression. I plan to explore more on this field.

Other than Python, I do learn C++ in my university course. I managed to make a simple food ordering system using C++ and now I'm learning Data Structure in C++. Besides, I also learn the basic web dev(HTML, CSS, and Javascript) and Flutter by attending workshops.

My passion is not only in technology but also in Japanese Culture. I also self-learn Japanese Language and I managed to pass the Japanese Language Proficiency Test(JLPT) N1, which is the highest level for that category during my high school time. I'm now the president of the Japanese Language Club in my university and am also one of the members of the Japanese Cultural Centre(JCC). On top of that,I also joined a few online cultural exchange events with Japanese Students from the country itself. My greatest ambition at the moment is to pursue my master's degree in Japan.
